Summer of TensorFlow
Jun 22 2017

One of my projects for this summer has been to learn TensorFlow. In my experience, I learn best when writing things out and thoroughly explaining them to myself. As a result, I've been working on a series of tutorials walking through different TensorFlow models. Most won't be entirely original, I plan on finding tutorials/code and annotating to a level that gives me a good understanding. Hopefully they might be helpful to others as well. To check out what's been posted in the series, see the Summer of Tensorflow tag.
